F.A.Q

  • What is ATOM CLUB Stand for?

    ATOM CLUB stand for Artificially Thinking cOded Machines.

  • Which Department Does ATOM CLUB beongs to?

    ATOM CLUB belongs to Department of Information Science and Engineering.

  • When was ATOM established and came into existence?

    ATOM CLUB established on November 2017, But it came into existence on 12-MAR-2018.

  • What is the moto of ATOM CLUB?

    The main moto of ATOM CLUB is to encourage students to take part in Technical Events, Cultural events, and motivate students to bring up their new ideas. It will conduct kinds of competition and workshops for the students in order to share some good amount of knowledge and ideas equally.

  • How it operates the events?

    It is a systematic process which plans for every event efficiently from the root of the system. It allocates well skilled speakers for the event and it also certifies the participants with a valid certificate for their participation.

  • Why you want to take part in it?

    It is also a good platform to gain good knowledge in the tech-part and also provides good opportunity for a competitors to take part in it and to bring up their ideas.
